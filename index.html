About Me

I'm an entry-level web developer with a background in Information and Communication Technology and a PGCE in Secondary Computer Science Teaching. My foundation in both technical development and education gives me a unique ability to communicate complex ideas clearly and work effectively in collaborative environments.

Having worked internationally and now based in Wexford, Ireland, I bring a global perspective, adaptability, and a strong work ethic. I'm currently seeking opportunities in web development—both within Ireland and remotely—where I can grow my skills and contribute to meaningful, user-focused projects.

My Skills

Design & Development

I began learning to code at 18 and have continued to grow my skills by building small applications and websites that apply principles of color theory, typography, and user interface/user experience (UI/UX) design. I’m proficient in Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. Most recently, I completed the Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp on Udemy, which strengthened my knowledge across both front-end and back-end technologies.

Communication Skills

Throughout my career, I’ve developed strong communication and problem-solving skills through roles in retail, call centers, and as a technical support engineer—assisting customers in person, over the phone, and via email. I also spent three years teaching English in Asia, where I planned and delivered engaging lessons to diverse groups of students. This experience strengthened my ability to communicate complex ideas clearly, adapt quickly, and manage time and expectations—skills that directly support my work in web development, particularly in collaborative environments and user-centered design.
